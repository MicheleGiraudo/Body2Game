Our games
The game room is a virtual environment, created within a video game designed for the rehabilitation of people with multiple sclerosis.
It is colorful, dynamic, and designed to be intuitive and motivating, where every activity combines fun and motor training.
Inside it, there are four games:
1. Gift Grabber
2. Wave Runner
3. Sky Fury
4. Clean Sheet
This virtual game room transforms rehabilitation into an interactive and engaging experience, where every movement becomes part of the game.
